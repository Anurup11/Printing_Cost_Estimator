About This System

This platform is built around MIS-based costing, helping teams estimate job costs with operational consistency and traceable logic.

It combines process-based costing with material-based costing so estimators can model production costs from prepress through final finishing.

Designed for printing and packaging industries, it supports practical production workflows and clear cost control decisions.
