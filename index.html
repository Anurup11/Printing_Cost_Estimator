About This System

This platform uses an MIS-oriented costing method to bring consistency, traceability, and accuracy to estimation workflows.

It combines process-based and material-based costing so teams can understand how each production step contributes to total job cost.

Built for printing and packaging operations, it supports smarter pricing decisions and better planning before production begins.

FAQs About the System

How does the system calculate cost?

The estimator combines material, setup, machine, finishing, and wastage logic to produce an end-to-end job cost estimate.

Can I save my account details locally?

Yes. The signup and login use local browser storage so you can access your account on this computer.

Do I need to be a printing expert?

No. The system is designed for printing and packaging teams to enter simple production inputs and review cost outputs.

Is my login information secure?

Credentials are stored locally in your browser and are only available on this device. For sensitive use, consider a dedicated secure solution.
